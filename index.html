Active · 2026Reinforcement LearningLab project · team of 3

RLPD — Offline-to-Online RL, extended to humanoids

Reproduction and extension of RLPD (Ball et al., ICML 2023) in PyTorch with Minari offline datasets: symmetric 50/50 sampling, LayerNorm critics, large ensembles at high UTD. Reproduced on three MuJoCo locomotion suites, then pushed to Humanoid-v5 with ablations over sampling ratio, ensemble size and update-to-data ratio.

entry 03 / 06
In progressHybrid architecturesLanguage modelling

Mamba-2 × attention — a hybrid LM ratio study

A 160M-parameter hybrid LM interleaving Mamba-2 SSM blocks with sparse causal attention (the Jamba pattern), trained on FineWeb-Edu at matched FLOPs. Mapping the attention:SSM ratio (1:3 / 1:7 / 1:15) against perplexity, throughput and KV-cache memory at 32K context — the 1:7 mix currently holds full-attention perplexity within ~3% at a fraction of the cache.

UNETR — 3D abdominal segmentation

Volumetric segmentation of 13 abdominal organs on the BTCV dataset with a UNETR (transformer-encoder U-Net). DICOM/NIfTI preprocessing with Hounsfield-unit windowing; class imbalance handled with Dice-Cross-Entropy loss to a mean Dice of 0.82.

En→Hi translation — a 43M-param Transformer, by hand

Encoder-decoder Transformer written from scratch in PyTorch and trained on 500k AI4Bharat Samanantar pairs: byte-level BPE, Noam schedule with label smoothing, length-normalised GNMT beam search. 16.93 BLEU / 41.58 chrF++ on a frozen 5k test set, shipped with a Gradio demo streaming live cross-attention heatmaps.

In progressInterpretabilitySafety

Sparse autoencoders — tracing circuits in a small LM

Training sparse autoencoders over the residual stream of a small open LM to decompose activations into monosemantic features, then circuit-tracing induction and indirect-object-identification behaviour. Early dictionaries recover interpretable features at ~78% auto-interp scores on the probed layers.
